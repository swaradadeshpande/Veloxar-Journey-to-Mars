Crossing the Cosmic Void

For 259 days, Veloxar hurtles through interplanetary space at 33,400 km/h. The crew contends with cosmic radiation, microgravity, and the psychological weight of deep isolation — 300 million kilometres from home.

Phase 06 — Legacy

Pioneers of the Red Planet

Before Veloxar, humanity sent robotic emissaries to pave the way. Explore the monumental achievements of India's ISRO and NASA that unlocked the secrets of Mars.

ISRO

Mangalyaan (MOM)

Launched: Nov 2013 | Arrived: Sep 2014

The Mars Orbiter Mission marked India's first interplanetary venture. Achieving orbit on its maiden attempt—a global first—MOM exceeded its 6-month designed lifespan by orbiting for nearly 8 years, mapping Martian surface features and morphology with its multi-color camera.

  • Most cost-effective Mars mission (~$74M)
  • First Asian nation to reach Mars orbit
  • Captured full-disc images of Mars
NASA

Perseverance & Ingenuity

Landed: Feb 2021 (Mars 2020)

Designed to seek signs of ancient microbial life, Perseverance roves Jezero Crater gathering rock and soil samples. It was accompanied by the Ingenuity Mars Helicopter, which completed 72 historic flights, proving powered, controlled flight on another planet.

  • First powered flight on Mars (Ingenuity)
  • Extracted oxygen from Martian CO₂ (MOXIE)
  • Caching samples for future Earth return
